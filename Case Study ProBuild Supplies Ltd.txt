Case Study: ProBuild Supplies Ltd

ProBuild Supplies Ltd, enterprise based in the UK that specialises in the retail and rental of building materials and construction tools. Operates from a single centralised site which includes a retail storefront, a warehouse for inventory storage and a tool hire division. Spans 3 key domains: 
- retail sales of building materials and tools
- tool hire services
- customer loyalty and trade finance partnerships

Key Business Processes

Actors: customers, probuild, warehouse, logistics team, tool hire process, FinTrust UK
Tasks(Activities):
hard goals(services): Products, delivery, rental tools(customers can rent them from half a day to multiple weeks), Trade card
soft goals(qualities): maintenance and servicing( safety, compliance, operational readiness)
resources(entities to be delivered): payment(can be done through cash card or interest free finance(available to eligible members))

tool hire process:
- customer checks availability
- booking is confirmed and payment or deposit is taken 
- tools are issued with a return date and a condition log
- upon return tools are inspected
- tools are sent for servicing to an external maintenance partner(FixPro)

warehouse handles:
- receipt of incoming goods from suppliers
- stocking and binning of tools and materials
- order fulfilment for both walk-in and online customers
- tool hire returns and temporary storage before maintenance collection

Inventory management system(IMS) integrates with the POS(Point-of-Sale) system to maintain stock levels. Staff perform daily stock checks and a weekly cycle count

Benefits of Trade card:
- Access to interest-free financing
- Discount of up to 10% based on loyalty points
- Priority tool hire booking during peak seasons
- Points are accrued per pound spent and can be used to increase the discount band; points reset annually

FinTrust process: 
- Trade card member opts for finance at checkout
- Credit application is processed by fintrust
- fintrust pays probuild the full amount upfront; customer can take the items immediately
- customer has to repay fintrust the full amount in equal monthly installment over a period of 6/12 months
