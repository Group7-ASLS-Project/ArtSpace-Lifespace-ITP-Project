Who We Are

A Registered Charity Reviving Forgotten Spaces

ArtSpace LifeSpace (ASLS) specialises in repurposing underused buildings into vibrant community arts centres, working closely with artists, property owners, local authorities and communities.

Our 'meanwhile use' approach provides security and cost savings for property owners, which can be reinvested back into supporting the local artist and creative economy.
