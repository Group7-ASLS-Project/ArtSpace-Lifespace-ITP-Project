The Island

A community arts venue in the Old Bridewell Police Station

Based in the Old Bridewell Police Station in Bristol city centre, The Island hosts over 100 resident artists with a Circus Training Space, music studios, Dance Studios and a beautiful art gallery.

Sparks Bristol

Explore what a greener, fairer and more creative future could look like

A partnership between Artspace Lifespace and Global Goals Centre, breathing new life into the former M&S building. A hub for local artists, a reuse shop, repair services, and workshops on climate, equality and wellbeing.
