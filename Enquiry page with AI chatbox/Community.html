What We Offer

  • Consultancy and creative solutions to support both meanwhile and longer-term development with community and creativity at its heart

Our Mission

Artspace Lifespace are creative placemakers, curating and caring for a diverse range of spaces to create welcoming places that reflect the community's identity and values, where artists can create, collaborate and present their work.

Our organisation exists to promote art for the benefit of the public by establishing and maintaining facilities for artists from a variety of artistic practices to create work, collaborate, and present and perform that work for the public.

  • Provide access to affordable, inclusive creative spaces
  • Support artists to create, collaborate and present their work
  • Transform underused buildings into vibrant cultural places
  • Promote art for the benefit of the public
  • Strengthen communities through creativity, culture and placemaking
